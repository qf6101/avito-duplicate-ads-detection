parameter tuning¶

  • grid search and fit on leaderboard
  • max_depth: 5 -> 10 -> 15(auc increase in local, auc decrease in leaderboard)
  • subsample=0.8, colsample_bytree=0.8
  • min_child_weight = 1
  • learning_rate=0.05, n_estimators=1000
